// about the hackathon

The most open hackathon
you'll ever join.

We believe creativity matters more than rules. Submit any project, any size, in any language. There are zero barriers to entry.

⚡

Any Language, Any Project

Python, JavaScript, C++, Rust, Scratch — we literally accept everything. A terminal game, a web app, a script that prints "Hello World" — it all counts.

👥

Solo or Team — No Limits

Work alone or bring your whole classroom. No cap on team size. Collaborate however you like, then submit together via the Google Form.

📅

Submit Anytime

The hackathon runs for 3 months. Submit on day one or the final day — no penalties for late submission. You have until May 24, 2026.

🚫

No Registration Needed

Skip the signup forms. Just go straight to the Google Form when you're ready to submit. No account, no pre-registration — nothing.

🏫

Open to All Students

Whether you're in school, college, or university — anywhere in the world — you're welcome. Unlimited participants. The more, the merrier.

🌱

Big or Small, Just Ship It

Don't overthink it. A 50-line script is as valid as a full-stack app. The point is to build and share. Creativity and effort matter most.

// frequently asked questions

Got questions?
We've got answers.

Do I need to register beforehand?

Nope! There's no registration required. Just submit your project directly through the Google Form whenever you're ready.

What can I submit?

Absolutely anything — a web app, a CLI tool, a game, a script, a Discord bot, a machine learning model. Any language, any framework, any size.

Can I work in a team?

Yes! Teams of any size are welcome. There's no minimum or maximum. Submit together via the Google Form and list all team members.

What if I submit late?

No penalties for late submissions! As long as you submit before May 24, 2026, you're good. We designed the hackathon to be stress-free.

How will projects be judged?

Projects will be evaluated on creativity, effort, and problem-solving. Don't worry — there's no "boring" project here. Just build and submit.

Who can participate?

Any student, anywhere in the world. School student, college student, university student — all are welcome. Unlimited participants.

When will I get my certificate?

Certificates will be distributed to all participants after the submission deadline closes on May 24, 2026. Winners will be announced separately.

I have more questions — who do I contact?

Reach out to us at finvio.app@gmail.com and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
